Get Started

Forget about queues

With CLup, which is the acronym of "Customers Line Up", going to your favorite grocery shopping in this period of social distancing could not be even simpler.

Register, Book, Shop!

You can book a ticket in your favorite store. It's simple! Follow these steps:

Register to CLup application (or sign in if already registered),
choose the store where you want to go,
put yourself in queue,
show the QR Code to the store attendant!
Let's Go!

If I don't have a smartphone?

Don't worry! Each store will have a point of "manual" ticket retrieving. Simply go there and ask them to be queued up! It's free and really simple!
